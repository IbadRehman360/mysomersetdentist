Raj Sinha, D.M.D., ICOI

Dr. Raj Sinha graduated form Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) and received his Bachelors in 1987. In 1991, he graduated from the University of Pennsylvania and received his Doctorate of Dental Medicine. Dr. Sinha completed his Bachelors and Dental Doctorate in 6 years instead of 8, making him the youngest doctor in his graduating class.

Dr. Sinha has participated in numerous courses and seminars to expand his dental education, including the Dawson Center for Advanced Dental Studies (for bite and TMJ disorders), Dental Implantology, Periodontology (gum disease) and Sedation dentistry. In addition, Dr. Sinha has advanced training from New York University and was a clinical instructor at NYU Dental School. He is a fellow with the International Congress of Oral Implantologists.

Joanna
Hrymoc-Sinha, D.M.D.

Meet Dr. Joanna Hrymoc-Sinha. After earning her Bachelors of Science degree from Rutgers University in 2002, Dr. Hrymoc-Sinha went on to graduate from the University of Medicine and Dentistry in 2006, earning her Doctorate of Dental Medicine. She has established a reputation as a top dentist in the community, earning the title of Jersey Choice Top Dentist and being featured in Jersey Monthly Magazine.

But Dr. Hrymoc's expertise extends far beyond her private practices at Somerset & Hillsborough Smiles. As a Clinical Assistant Professor at the New York University College of Dentistry's Full Mouth Rehabilitation program, she teaches other dentists how to diagnose, treat and manage difficult cases involving conventional and implant-supported full-mouth rehabilitation. She is honored to be the first female faculty at the Full Mouth Reconstruction program at NYU.
